### Simple Pleasures

- Airplane bathroom door latch
    - Nothing like good hardware. They don’t make em like they used to. airplanes have good / tactile hardware (the sound of an overhead bin latching, the twist to open air nozzles, the folding door in the bathroom, the latch on the door that turns the light on)
- Grocery shopping with family / shopping in a new market
    - Asian and Mexican markets are always a treasure trove of novel snacks and food. I love that the butchers sell different things than your average Kroger. We will see more localized grocery stores over time when climate change impacts begin.
- The pleasure of hugging / holding a dog when the world feels like it’s hanging on by a thread
    - Even through a carrier, he was a comfort to me on the plane
- Owls at night
    - I haven’t seen owls before. Still haven’t, but I got to hear them and see their shadow.
    - One launched off an antennae and onto a palm tree
