## Notes

- I always look back at these pieces from the perspective of my producer instead of myself
    - Really... I do like these pieces. I still do. So why do I feel like I need to do better than these
- It amazes me how formless the pieces feel to me now. How did I come up with this stuff back then without feeling tied to a certain structure. How did I think of the music making process? How did I decide “this sounds good” or “this needs something else”?
    - Mesford is so interesting because of how organic the structure feels. And feels like it has the proper rise and fall that I struggle with now
- Hilarious that I have both Zach and Zach J as artists on this
- If I were to do something differently, I would have advocated for a more “produced” sound. Characterize the production of the sound more, as the songs themselves don’t necessarily feel _as_ potent as I would wish them to be. Not necessarily strong enough to last on their own.
- Market’s little rising motif is so questioning and mysterious. Love it. The left hand I don’t love.
    - I do still find the chord changes in the left hand interesting, and the concept of the turnaround quite good too. But I feel like it could be played a little more interestingly
